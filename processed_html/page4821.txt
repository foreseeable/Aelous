Baking for other people makes us feel good. My friends at  Bob s Red Mill  sent me an article all about the  psychology of baking  and so much of it describes me (and probably many of you) perfectly. Baking is my way of letting you know that you are important to me and that I care. And then Bob s challenged me to create a care package of baked goods for someone I care about. I had to give this some thought, because keto and low carb baked goods are often more fragile and contain more moisture than conventional goodies. So getting them to a far away friend intact and without spoiling means you have to pick something that s a little on the drier side. I ve done biscotti for people many times, but this time I went with scones.
